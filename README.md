# Arrabet Knowledge Graph

This repository contains the official **knowledge graph of Arrabet Holding** and its entities.

Arrabet is a tech investment holding building an integrated African tech ecosystem across:
- Digital Build
- Solutions Integration
- Data & AI
- Sovereign AI
- Creative & Digital Branding
- Venture Building
- Talent & Capacity Building

## Entities

The graph currently includes the following entities:

1. **Mobiblanc** — Digital Build Factory  
   Web & mobile apps, digital products, selfcare platforms, UX/UI, nearshore engineering.

2. **Foster Processing** — Solutions Integration  
   Core banking, ERP/CRM, cloud, DevOps, automation, enterprise integration.

3. **The Frontline Unit** — Data & AI Factory  
   Data platforms, AI use cases, LLMs, automation for telecom, finance, retail, public sector.

4. **Aiscapade** — Sovereign AI Lab  
   Sovereign AI models, hybrid AI architectures, MLOps, responsible & auditable AI.

5. **AsteriCX** — Public Sector Digital Experience  
   Citizen experience, public digital services, public-sector reporting and automation.

6. **Arrabet Academy** — Talent & Capacity Building  
   Training programs in data, AI, product, cloud, software engineering; talent pipeline for the group.

7. **Palpz** — Creative & Digital Branding Studio  
   Brand identity, rebranding, UI/UX, design systems, motion design, digital content & campaigns.

8. **Arrabet.Ventures** — Venture Studio & Investment Arm  
   Venture building, SaaS incubation, early-stage investment, go-to-market structuring, AI-first startup support.
